Steps to make a sandwhich:

  1. Prepare all your ingredients especially the most important one (BREAD).
  2. Take the first piece of bread.
  3. Put your chosen spread, cheese, vegetables, meats, or dressings in any order.
  4. Put on top the second/last piece of bread.
  5. Lastly the important step, ENJOY EATING!
